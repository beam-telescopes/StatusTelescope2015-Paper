\abstract{
The status of the $\Datura$ beam telescope at DESY is summarised and the performance with respect to resolution as a function of the position along the beam, the beam energy and other parameters is shown. 
The pointing resolution using a 6\,GeV $\e$-beam at the centre of the telescope is $\unit{3.4}{\upmu\meter}$. %FIXME check number
As expected, the resolution improves with increasing beam energies. 
An optimal threshold of the used MIMOSA26 sensors is found at a signal-to-noise figure of about six, with noise hits deteriorating the resolution towards lower and inefficiencies towards higher thresholds. 
}
